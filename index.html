Common symptoms of hidden data complexity

In most cases, the root cause is data that is tightly coupled to individual applications — instead of managed as an independent shared layer.

📉

Different reports show different numbers for the same metric

⏳

New analytics or reporting requests take weeks to implement

🔄

📊

⚠️

🧩

The Concept

What is Enterprise Data Independence?

Enterprise Data Independence means that business data is decoupled from specific applications and managed as a shared organizational asset.

When organizations reach this state, data governance becomes a business capability — not an IT bottleneck.

♻️

Data reused across systems

One definition, used everywhere — no duplication, no drift between teams.

⚡

Faster, more reliable analytics

Reports are consistent because the data foundation is shared and stable.

🔧

New systems without breaking processes

New applications plug in without disrupting existing data flows and reports.
